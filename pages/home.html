About Me

I started my career in banking, working in business sales and later as a personal banker. I loved helping clients understand their options and guiding them toward financial solutions that truly supported their goals. After banking, I joined the City of Charlotte as a 311 representative, where I developed strong communication skills and learned how to problem solve quickly while supporting citizens across the city. As of today, I am a coding apprentice, building my technical skills and exploring opportunities in the tech industry. I’m passionate about learning, improving every day, and creating solutions that make people’s lives easier. Outside of work, I enjoy traveling, discovering new places, and finding balance between motherhood and pursuing my career goals.
