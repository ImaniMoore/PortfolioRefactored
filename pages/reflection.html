Professional Development

During my professional career, especially through my background in banking, I discovered that I thrive in fast paced environments. I enjoy learning quickly, adapting to new challenges, and taking initiative when priorities shift. Over time, I’ve realized how much I value workplaces where growth and continuous learning are part of the culture. Now, as I transition into a tech role, I’m excited to apply that same mindset staying curious, picking up new tools and concepts quickly, and contributing to projects that make a real impact.

Technical Development

As I continue growing in my technical journey, I’ve realized that many programming languages share similar concepts. I enjoy recognizing those connections and using them to understand new tools more quickly. It’s empowering to know that with the right logic and structure, I can use different languages to build the solutions I imagine. So far, I’ve learned HTML and CSS, developed projects using JavaScript, and begun working with Node.js. I’ve also used Sass for cleaner styling and am currently learning Tailwind CSS to build responsive, modern interfaces more efficiently.

Personal Development

I’ve come to realize that I genuinely love learning and developing myself in new ways. Whether it’s improving my skills, strengthening my mindset, or gaining new perspectives, personal growth is something I truly value. Investing in myself has become a meaningful form of self care. It helps me stay grounded, motivated, and confident as I continue moving forward.
